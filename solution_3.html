Wildlife

Wildlife refers to all the flora and fauna, including both plants and animals, in their natural habitat. It encompasses a wide range of species, from tiny insects to large mammals, and plays a crucial role in maintaining the balance of ecosystems.

We need the tonic of wildness...At the same time that we are earnest to explore and learn all things, we require that all things be mysterious and unexplorable, that land and sea be indefinitely wild, unsurveyed and unfathomed by us because unfathomable. We can never have enough of nature.
